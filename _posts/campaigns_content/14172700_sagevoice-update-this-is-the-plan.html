Mental health update

I try to be honest about how things are going in my brain.  I mentioned in the last email that I was reprioritizing constantly.  Well, that hit a fever pitch last week.  It totally sucked.  You know when you’re sleeping and your mind is racing and you have fever dreams in which you’re stressed out? (really hoping that’s not just me)


Okay, well I was doing that while awake, but with a startup. Everything seemed urgent, I kept switching what I was doing, feeling guilty, and generally stressed that I should be doing something else.  Made progress, but was not in a good head space for a couple days.


Feeling better now.  Friday I had a customer call and came out of it with a renewed sense of purpose and direction.

This section is rapidly evolving…I started just being amazed by stock photo libraries (and the business of stock photograph).  In the last few weeks, I showed how you can turn photos into videos, and, last week, change the race of the people involved.  I am so impressed that these companies aren’t letting AI destroy their business, but went all in: using it to provide very compelling new offerings.
